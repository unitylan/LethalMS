Featured Build
Golf GTI

A fully modified Golf GTI, pushing 430HP with top-tier upgrades including a high-performance turbo, ECU remap, and custom exhaust system, offering both track-ready performance and street driving excitement.

  • High-performance turbocharger for increased power and optimized airflow.
  • Custom ECU tuning to maximize engine output while maintaining reliability.
  • Performance exhaust system for improved airflow, reduced backpressure, and a more aggressive sound.
  • Custom cold air intake to enhance throttle response and sustain power under heavy driving.
  • Upgraded suspension with adjustable coilovers for better handling and stability.
  • Enhanced braking system with larger rotors and multi-piston calipers for improved stopping power.
  • Upgraded cooling system with an intercooler and additional radiators to prevent overheating during high-performance use.
